Global
Research Center

To drive sustainable tourism, STGC is launching a Global Research Center for Climate, Nature & Communities. The Center will hire top researchers and collaborate with leading universities and organizations to provide actionable insights, tools, and solutions.

The strategy for the Center has been developed through a collaborative approach and a robust validation process, with over 300 leaders with expertise in sustainability and/or Travel & Tourism from across the world, including governments and leadings international organizations.

The Center aims to partner with 100 universities from diverse regions around the world by 2030 - North America, Europe, Asia-Pacific, Latin America, Africa and MENA - and to engage with international organizations and industry associations.
